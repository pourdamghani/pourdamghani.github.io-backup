About Me

II'm a direct Ph.D. student at the INET group at the Technical University of Berlin, Germany, working under the supervision of Prof. Stefan Schmid. Previously I was a researcher at the University of Vienna and completed research internships in IST Austria and CUHK Hong Kong. I got my B.Sc from the Sharif University of Technology, Iran, and I am interested in algorithm design, with applications in networks and blockchains.
